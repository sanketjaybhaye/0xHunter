💡 Common DNS Tips for Bug Hunters
Subdomain Takeover Check:
Look for CNAME pointing to services that show "NoSuchBucket", "There is no app at this address", etc.
DNS Zone Transfer:
dig axfr @ns1.target.com target.com
SPF/DMARC Check:
Query TXT records for v=spf1 and _dmarc. Missing = phishing opportunity.
Wildcard DNS:
If *.target.com resolves, compare with non-existent.target.com to detect wildcards.
